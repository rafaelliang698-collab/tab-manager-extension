TabFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Summary: TabFlow does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device.

Data Collection

TabFlow does not collect any personal data from users. We do not track, monitor, or record your activity.

Local Storage Only

All data stored by TabFlow (theme preference, grid layout settings, and other user preferences) is stored locally in your browser using the chrome.storage API and never leaves your device. This data is:

  • Not transmitted to any external server
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Not used for analytics or tracking
  • Only accessible to you and the TabFlow extension

Tab Information

TabFlow reads your open browser tabs solely to display them in the extension's tab manager interface. This information:

  • Is never sent to any external server
  • Is never stored on our systems
  • Is only processed locally within your browser
  • Is only visible to you in the extension interface

Permissions

TabFlow requests only two essential permissions:

  • tabs - to read and manage your open tabs
  • storage - to save your preferences locally on your device

These permissions are used exclusively for the extension's core functionality and are not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

TabFlow does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services. No data is sent outside of your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
